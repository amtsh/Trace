How it works

You leave a thread. Trace keeps the stack.

Between meetings, messages, and browser tabs, the trail goes cold. Trace groups the apps, windows, and files you used, then lets you open that stack again.

1

Watches the surface

Foreground apps, window titles, and document URLs via Accessibility. No screenshots. No keystrokes.

2

Builds sessions

Related work becomes cards with duration and a short sentence you can scan at a glance.

3

Restores the stack

One click reopens the apps, files, and links from any session when you’re ready to continue.

Privacy

Private by design. Fully offline.

Built for people who need context without surveillance. Your history stays on this Mac, and fades on its own.

  • No screenshots or keystrokes Titles and paths only, never what you typed or read.
  • Local SQLite storage Nothing is uploaded. No account required.
  • Auto-deletes after 7 days Short memory by default. Less residue, less worry.
